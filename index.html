About Cardalordz

The mission of CardaLordz is to bring all world religions together through a unique spiritual metaverse. Our project’s CNFTs function as avatars and admit owners into the metaverse where they will be able to study the deity on their CNFT.

Each CNFT will feature a deity with varying passages from their religious text, allowing for users to both further their knowledge of the deity and to create card variations respectfully.
