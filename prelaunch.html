Stability

We are seeking to prevent a clogging of the chain. Early NFTs had the problem of gas fees and transaction times skyrocketing during launch.

Solution

We believe by using Cardano as our primary currency, instead of Ethereum, we can avoid the network clogging that comes with the Ethereum NFT boom. Cardano is still new to the NFT space with many of the elements of our project being unique to Cardano based NFTs.
